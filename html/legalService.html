General Legal Service

Specialized Practice Areas

Our Corporate Team supports various enterprises, emerging start-up companies and financial institutions in their development and investment activities. Our team handles all aspects of complex domestic and cross-border corporate transactions. Understanding the industry and the day-to-day issues faced by our clients is critical to our combined success. We help our clients with cross-border M&A (public and private), including: inception, planning and due diligence, deal execution, post-merger integrations, joint ventures, investment funds, venture capital and corporate governance.

We advise our clients on data protection and privacy requirements, compliance, policies and procedures. Our data protection and privacy practice specialises in the legal, regulatory and commercial aspects of data protection and privacy, in both ‘onshore’ United Arab Emirates (UAE) and the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC). We advise on the use, processing, and transfer of personal data and sensitive personal data through various forums, such as via electronic communications, physical transfers, filing systems, the media, and other technology or software.

Our clients range from start-ups to emerging multinationals. We work with our clients locally, internationally and across borders. Our global reach and local knowledge mean that we can partner with clients to drive consistency, deliver cost savings and help them identify and manage their priorities and risk across multiple locations. We offer a range of legal services for international and regional employers in all sectors and for the employment relationship. We advise on immigration and residency rules, Ministry of Labor compliance, staff benefits and incentive schemes, redundancy and termination management, end of service gratuity settlements, drafting and review of service contracts and all other kinds of employment contract documentation. The team also works on the employment aspects of business registrations, business transfers and restructuring. Additionally, we provide support to our dispute resolution colleagues when employment matters become contentious.
